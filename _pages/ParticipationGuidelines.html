Participation guidelines

The Build a Binder workshops respect Mozilla's MozFest participation guidelines. These guidelines cover our behaviours as participants, organisers and facilitators.

How to treat each other

  • Be respectful and value each other's ideas, styles and viewpoints
  • Be direct but professional; we caanot withold hard truths
  • Be inclusive and help new perspectives be heard
  • Appreciate and accomodate our many cultural practices, attitues and beliefs
  • Be open to learning from others
  • Lead by example and match your actions with your words
